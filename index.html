About Us

RedCap Ventures

Redcap Ventures is a company specializing in professional aviation services. We offer training in Aviation Safety, Occupational Health & Safety, and corporate development, along with consultancy services in aviation human resources, mental health programs, and FODC programs. The company was founded by experienced professionals aiming to provide effective training and consultation to individuals and organizations in various sectors.
